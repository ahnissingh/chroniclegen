The Lazy Squirrel and the Diligent Ant

Once upon a time in a lush green forest, there lived a lazy squirrel named Sammy. Sammy loved to spend his days lounging in the sun and munching on acorns, but he always put off collecting food for the winter.

One sunny day, while stretching out on a branch, Sammy noticed an ant working diligently. This ant was named Andy, and he was carrying tiny pieces of food back to his nest. Sammy couldn't help but laugh. "Why do you work so hard, little ant? Winter is far away!" he chirped.

Andy paused, wiped his brow, and replied, "I believe in being prepared, Sammy. When winter comes, food will be scarce. I want to make sure I'm ready when the snow covers the ground."

Sammy shrugged and returned to his sunbathing, confident in his carefree approach to life. Days turned to weeks, and as autumn began to chill the air, Sammy finally decided he should gather some food. With a shiver down his spine, he ventured out to find acorns, but to his surprise, most had already been collected by the busy ants and other animals preparing for winter.

As snow began to blanket the forest, Sammy found himself scurrying about in despair. He remembered Andy’s advice too late. Day after day, Sammy watched as Andy and his fellow ants feasted on their stored food while he went hungry, trying to nibble on what little he could find beneath the snow.

Finally, Sammy approached Andy, who was kind enough to offer him a few crumbs. "Thank you for your generosity, Andy. I should have listened to you about preparing for winter. I have learned my lesson now."

From that day on, Sammy worked hard every autumn to gather enough food, ensuring he would never face a hungry winter again.

And so, Sammy and Andy became good friends, sharing their time and food with one another in the seasons that followed.
